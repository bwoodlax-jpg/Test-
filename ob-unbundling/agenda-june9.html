                BONUS: Leveraging Agentic AI to Accelerate This Project
                An afterthought worth exploring — how AI could work alongside the consulting team
                1

                AI-Assisted FAQ Drafting

                When a physician submits an intake question, an AI agent pre-drafts a coding answer using the AMA CPT 2027 guidelines. The consulting team reviews and approves — cutting response time from days to hours. The AI handles the first 80%, the expert handles the final 20%.

                2

                Education Script Co-Writing

                Use AI to generate a first-draft script for each video module based on the AMA guidelines and the playbook content. The consulting team refines tone, accuracy, and clinical nuance. Cuts script-writing time from weeks to days.

                3

                Physician Documentation Reviewer

                An AI agent that a physician pastes their de-identified note into, and it tells them: "Based on your documentation, you qualify for 99214. Here's what you'd need to add to support 99215." Instant, on-demand documentation coaching.

                4

                Coding Scenario Generator

                AI generates unlimited new coding scenarios for the scenario library and attestation quizzes — varying complexity, phase of care, comorbidities. The consulting team validates them. Ensures the FAQ and training library never go stale.

                5

                AMA / ACOG Change Monitor

                An agentic AI that monitors AMA, ACOG, CMS, and SMFM websites for any updates to 2027 coding guidance and sends the consulting team an alert with a plain-language summary. No more manually checking for updates.

                6

                Post-Go-Live Audit Assistant

                After January 1, 2027, AI analyzes claim patterns across the department — flagging physicians who may be undercoding (e.g., always billing 99213 when the documentation supports 99214) and generating a personalized coaching report.

## What Drives Etsy Sales (monitor weekly)

1. **Search ranking** — are our keywords showing up?
   - Check: Etsy Shop Manager → Stats → Search Terms
2. **Click-through rate** — are people clicking our listing?
   - Target: 2%+ CTR from search
   - Fix low CTR: improve main photo
3. **Conversion rate** — are viewers buying?
   - Target: 1-3% conversion
   - Fix low conversion: improve description, add reviews, lower price temporarily
4. **Reviews** — social proof drives sales
   - Target: 5+ reviews in first 30 days
   - Strategy: message every buyer asking for feedback

# Canva Build Guide — Step-by-Step Instructions
This tells you exactly what to do in Canva to produce each product.
I've written all the content. You're just assembling it.

---

## SETUP (Do this once — 10 minutes)

1. Log into Canva Pro at canva.com
2. Click **"Create a design"** → **"Custom size"**
3. Set size to **8.5 x 11 in** (US Letter) — use this for ALL products
4. Name your first design: **"Teen Money Mastery Bundle"**

### Brand Colors to set up (save these in Canva's Brand Kit):
- **Dark Navy:** #1a1a2e
- **Neon Green:** #00ff87
- **Electric Yellow:** #FFD60A
- **White:** #ffffff
- **Light Gray:** #f0f0f0
- **Orange accent:** #FF6B35
- **Purple accent:** #7B2FBE

### Fonts to use (Canva Pro has these):
- **Headlines:** "Space Grotesk" Bold or "Outfit" ExtraBold
- **Body text:** "Inter" Regular or "DM Sans" Regular
- **Accent/labels:** "Space Grotesk" Medium

## PRODUCT 1: Teen Money Mastery Bundle (12 pages)

**Layout:**

## EXPORTING YOUR FILES

When all pages are done:

1. Click **"Share"** → **"Download"**
2. Select **"PDF Print"** (NOT PDF Standard — Print gives higher quality)
3. Check **"Crop marks and bleed"** — OFF (not needed for digital)
4. Download
5. Rename the file: `teen-money-mastery-bundle.pdf` or `teen-side-hustle-starter-kit.pdf`
6. Upload this file to your Etsy listing as the digital download

---

## MAKING IT FILLABLE (for the Budget Tracker product)

After exporting from Canva as PDF:
1. Open the PDF in **Adobe Acrobat** (free version works)
2. Go to **Tools → Prepare Form**
3. Acrobat will auto-detect fields — review and clean up
4. Save as a new PDF: `teen-budget-tracker-fillable.pdf`
5. This is what you upload to Etsy

Alternative: Canva itself now has form fields in Pro — search "Add text input" in elements if you want to stay in Canva.

## PRODUCT PHOTOS FOR ETSY LISTINGS

You need 3-5 mockup images per listing. Here's how:

**Option A (Free):**
1. Go to **smartmockups.com** (free tier available)
2. Search "paper mockup" or "printable mockup"
3. Upload your PDF cover page as the image
4. Download the mockup — use as your Etsy listing photo

**Option B (In Canva):**
1. Search Canva templates for "product mockup"
2. Replace the placeholder with your PDF cover screenshot
3. Add text overlay: "INSTANT DOWNLOAD" badge in neon green

**Recommended mockup types per listing:**
- Photo 1: Cover flat lay (paper on desk)
- Photo 2: Multi-page fan spread (shows it's a full workbook)
- Photo 3: Close-up of one interior page (shows quality)
- Photo 4: "What's Included" graphic (list all pages visually)
- Photo 5: Lifestyle shot (teen at desk with the printed workbook)

## PAGE 8 — HUSTLE MINDSET PAGE

**Headline:** The 5 Rules of the Teen Entrepreneur

**Rule 1: Start Ugly**
Your first version will not be perfect. Do it anyway. You can improve it after you have customers. Waiting for perfect = never starting.

**Rule 2: Rejection Is Data**
Every "no" tells you something. Too expensive? Wrong audience? Wrong offer? Don't take it personally — take notes.

**Rule 3: Your Network Is Your Net Worth**
Tell everyone you know what you're doing. Most teens are too embarrassed. The ones who aren't — they get the customers.

**Rule 4: Profit Over Revenue**
$500 in sales with $400 in costs = $100 profit. $200 in sales with $10 in costs = $190 profit. Profit is what matters. Keep your costs low when starting.

**Rule 5: Reinvest Early**
Take 20-30% of what you earn and put it back into the business. Better tools, better materials, better marketing. It compounds just like investing does.
